Dogs, often referred to as man's best friend, have lived alongside humans for thousands of years. 
From working breeds that herd livestock or assist in search-and-rescue missions to loyal companions 
that provide emotional support, dogs play a vital role in human society. They are known for their 
remarkable sense of smell, high intelligence, and unwavering loyalty. Diverse in size, temperament, 
and appearance, the hundreds of distinct breeds reflect centuries of selective breeding. Whether they 
are active athletes or quiet lapdogs, the bond between humans and their canine companions continues to 
be one of the most enduring and cherished relationships in the animal kingdom.
